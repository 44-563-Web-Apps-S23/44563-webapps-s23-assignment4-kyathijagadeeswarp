MRS. BAUER.

Friends!

LANE.

Rotten trick to play on the boss, all right, puttin' that old maid up to get him pinched.

[_Absently._]

Was she an old maid?

LANE.

The cruelty-to-animals woman over there

[_waves his hand_]

--regular old crank. Nies

[H]

put her up to it all right.
